Resort Planet is a business that operates a family restaurant and a hotel in the village of Kmetovc in Gjilan.The resort is located in a rural area and employs people from the local community.The location and services of the resort offer clients a unique way to experience the nature of Kosovo. The restaurant is built from recycled stones and wood, which have been gathered to give the structure a traditional architectural style. The menu features a rich variety of traditional dishes prepared with fresh local ingredients to promote our heritage and cuisine.
